Formatting your paper

The full proceedings will be published by the IEEE Computer Society.

Therefore, all papers and other materials for the proceedings must be typeset to conform to IEEE conference style guidelines.

Instructions for this are available in the following formats: MS-Word Document | PS | PDF.

For MS-Word users, the instruction word document can be used as a basis for formatting your paper. Otherwise, the format.doc file can be used. This is also available in PS and PDF formats.

Latex users should download IEEE_CS_Latex.zip which contains the required style files.
