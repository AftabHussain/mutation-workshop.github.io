Registration & Hotel Information

Registration

The registration fee for Mutation 2000 is $100. Payment via credit card is preferred, VISA, MasterCard and American Express are accepted. The credit card will also serve to hold your room reservation (see below). If financial arrangements other than credit card are necessary please contact Rosemary Hoehler ([email protected], Phone: +1 732 699-5697, Fax: +1 732 336-6567) at Telcordia Technologies.

Those staying on for ISSRE 2000 need to register separately for ISSRE.

Hotel Arrangements

Mutation 2000 has arranged for a block of rooms at the DoubleTree Hotel, San Jose for Thursday, October 5 and Friday, October 6, 2000 at $159 per night if reserved before September 10, 2000. Reservations MUST be made through Mutation 2000 registration. Payment for rooms will be made directly, by the guest, to the hotel upon checkout. For those staying over for ISSRE their conference rate is not available prior to Saturday, October 7 and requires a separate reservation. We do ask that those staying over indicate this on your Mutation 2000 registration form so we can advise the hotel to attempt to minimize potential Saturday disruptions shifting to your ISSRE reservation.

Any changes/cancellations for Mutation 2000 (conference or hotel) must be submitted to Rosemary Hoehler ([email protected], Phone: +1 732 699-5697, Fax: +1 732 336-6567) at Telcordia Technologies.
